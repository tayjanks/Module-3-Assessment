Oquirrh Shadows in Utah!

Oquirrh Shadows is a neighborhood in West Jordan, Utah. Named aptly as it rests under the shadows of the Oquirrh Mountains! It's about 20 minutes away from down-town Salt Lake and about 20 minutes away from ski resorts!
